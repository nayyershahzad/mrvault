REGULATORY DEADLINE

CBAM is not coming.
It's already here.

The EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism requires all exporters of steel, cement, aluminium, fertilizers, electricity, and hydrogen to report embedded emissions. Textile manufacturers face indirect exposure through energy costs — and direct scope as the EU expands CBAM downstream.

Without a certified MRV system, your exports face carbon tariffs of €50–100+ per tonne CO₂. MRVault gives you the IPCC-grade calculation engine and immutable audit trail regulators demand.

Verification Workflow

From Raw Data to Verified Credits

The full MRV lifecycle — calculate, evidence, verify, mint — all in one platform.

1

Calculate Emissions

Plant engineer enters fuel, production data. IPCC Tier 1 engine calculates CO₂e and potential credits per stream.

2

Attach Evidence

Upload fuel invoices, meter readings, calibration certs. Each evidence type is stream-specific with category tagging.

3

VVB Reviews & Raises Findings

Independent auditor (SGS, Bureau Veritas, Earthood) reviews calculations and evidence. Raises CARs, CLs, or FARs.

4

Plant Responds

Plant manager addresses each finding with corrective action. Lifecycle: Open → Responded → Closed.

5

Approve & Mint to Blockchain

VVB approves. Credits minted as ERC-721 NFTs on Polygon with IPFS-anchored evidence. Immutable and tradeable.

VVB Expanded Verification View

Complete audit trail: calculation inputs, result summary, uploaded evidence with category badges, findings panel, and approve/reject controls.
